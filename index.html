About the Event

Attire

The attire for our wedding is semi-formal!
The ceremony will be held outdoors with an indoor reception on a Las Vegas summer night, so y’know dress accordingly.

Ceremony

Do your best to arrive by 6:30 p.m!
Once the ceremony starts we can’t let late arrivals in, and this venue is roughly 30 minutes from the strip, so please keep this in mind.

Cocktail Hour

While we take a few pictures the reception area will be open for drinks and appetizers.

Reception

The reception will be buffet style and we’ll eat, dance, and drink until 11 p.m. Then you don’t have to go home, but the party will be over and we’ll definitely be making our exit!
